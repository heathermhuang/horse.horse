Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

What we collect

When you submit a score to the leaderboard, we store:

- Your score and play session stats (used for anti-cheat validation)
- Your country, detected automatically from your IP region via Cloudflare
- A random session identifier (generated in your browser, not linked to your identity)

What we don't collect

We don't collect your name, email, IP address, or any personally identifiable information. We don't use cookies for tracking. We don't sell data to anyone.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand general usage patterns (page views, country-level traffic). Google's privacy policy applies to data collected by their scripts.

Data storage

Leaderboard data is stored in Cloudflare D1 (a serverless database). Only the top scores are retained; older entries are periodically pruned.

Data removal

Since we don't store identifying information, we can't look up "your" data. If you have concerns, contact us at h.im.

Children

Horse.Horse is a simple browser game. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we don't collect personal information from anyone, this is straightforward.

Changes

We may update this policy. The date at the top will reflect the latest revision.
